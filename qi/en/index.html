This map shows an estimate of the average IQ for each census tract (Setor Censit√°rio) in the IBGE 2022 Census.

To calculate the average IQ for each sector, a weighted average was used. This method considers the number of people of each race in the sector and the average IQ values associated with each race:

  • Asians: 103
  • Whites: 96
  • Mixed-race (pardos): 90
  • Blacks: 85
  • Indigenous: 84

Sources for the average values:

  • Lynn, Richard, and Tatu Vanhanen. IQ and the wealth of nations. Bloomsbury Publishing USA, 2002.
  • Herrnstein, Richard J., and Charles Murray. The bell curve: Intelligence and class structure in American life. Simon and Schuster, 2010.
